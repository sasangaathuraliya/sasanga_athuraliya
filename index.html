Dreams & Goals

Build a Formula Student Legacy

I dream of shaping a new era of student motorsport in Sri Lanka by helping build a stronger, more competitive Formula Student team under SAE CINEC. With the groundwork already laid by other pioneering universities, my focus is on elevating performance, engineering standards and international visibility through collaborative design, advanced CAD modelling, strategic project management and high-level engineering communication. This journey is about more than a car. It's about inspiring a generation of engineers to push boundaries with purpose.

Master Aerodynamics in Motorsport

Driven by my passion for racing and science, I’m committed to mastering vehicle aerodynamics through CFD simulation, flow visualization, and physical prototyping. I aim to analyse complex flow behaviour around wings, diffusers, and underbodies using tools like OpenFOAM, Ansys and Python-based data pipelines. My long-term goal is to work in elite motorsport environments such as Formula 1 and Endurance motorsport, where aerodynamic efficiency and creative problem-solving decide the outcome of every lap.

Achieve CAD & Surfacing Mastery

Design begins with geometry, but excellence comes from surfacing mastery. I am dedicating myself to the craft of Class A, B and C surfacing using tools like CATIA, Autodesk Alias and SOLIDWORKS. From initial sketches to manufacturable bodywork, I want to create forms that merge structural logic with aesthetic impact. Whether it’s a race car or a road car, my goal is to bring technical clarity and design harmony to every surface.

Become a Design Engineer at a Leading Automotive Manufacturer

One of my goals is to earn a place as a Design Engineer at a reputed automobile manufacturing company. I envision myself contributing to the future of mobility by designing intelligent, efficient, and emotionally engaging vehicles. With expertise in solid and surface modelling, mechanical integration, and production-oriented design, I want to be part of a team that shapes the next generation of automobiles from concept to the production line.

Engineer for Environmental Impact

As part of my journey, I’ve volunteered in impactful projects like the Plastic Extractor Boat, where engineering meets environmental responsibility. I believe in creating machines that not only perform but also serve communities and protect ecosystems. My dream is to work on innovations that solve environmental challenges through clean, locally relevant, and sustainable technology integrating systems thinking, practical engineering, and real-world empathy.
